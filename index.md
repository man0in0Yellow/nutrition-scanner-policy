## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you register and use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, profile photo (optional)
- **Health & Fitness Data**: Age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, workout frequency, dietary preferences
- **Nutritional Targets**: Daily calorie goals, macronutrient targets (protein, carbs, fat)

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we automatically collect certain information:

- **Usage Data**: App features used, screen views, session duration
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Performance Data**: App crashes, errors, load times
- **Analytics Data**: Feature usage patterns, user interactions

### Information From Your Device

With your permission, we may access:

- **Camera**: To take photos of meals for nutritional analysis
- **Photo Library**: To select existing food photos for analysis
- **Apple Health** (iOS only): To sync calorie and activity data (optional)

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Services**
   - Analyze food photos and estimate nutritional content
   - Track your daily calorie and nutrient intake
   - Store your meal history and progress

2. **Personalize Your Experience**
   - Calculate personalized nutritional recommendations
   - Adjust targets based on your goals and activity level
   - Provide customized meal insights

3. **Improve Our Services**
   - Analyze app usage to improve features
   - Fix bugs and enhance performance
   - Develop new functionalities

4. **Communicate With You**
   - Send important app updates
   - Provide customer support
   - Send optional push notifications (with your consent)
